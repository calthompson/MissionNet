About the City Mission

Our Mission: To provide help and hope to all people through the transforming power of God's love.

Our Vision: is to be a catalyst for positive change in Cleveland resulting in the radical transformation of individuals and communities for generations to come.

For more information, please visit: https://thecitymission.org

About This Site

This website was built and is maintained by The City Mission's Information Technology department. Its main purpose is to provide an internal point of access to the viewing and the posting of upcoming events, weekly prayer requests, and items for sale. It also allows TCM staff to access shared network folders and monthly newsletters.
